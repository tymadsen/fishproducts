SAFARI GEAR SLING:
This is our most popular gear sling, The shoulder area is 2" seatbelt webbing completely enshrouded in Sham-Pelt, sporting a Zebra, Dalmatian, Bovine, or Ocelot pattern. The racking area is rolled and sewn 1" tubular webbing for easy gear management. This gear sling is lightweight and comfy, without that bulky feeling of fleece slings. Sm, M, L. $12.00 ea.

 

DOUBLE GEAR SLING: (same one from our Big Wall etc. page)
Each side of the "Double Gear Sling" has two racking loops of rolled 1" tubular webbing for easy ,gear management. Exclusive clip off loops at the apex of the padded shoulder straps make racking at the anchor easier than ever. Just clip in the loops and the racks will hang in an oriented position for gear sorting. A replaceable, wide rear tie-in keeps the weight of the racks over the shoulders, rather than on the neck. The front attachment is of 1" tubular webbing and features a no-slip Fastex buck1e for quick and secure adjustment. New features include a stay-open front clip on each shoulder for your aiders.
One size fits just about everyone. $49.00 ea.
